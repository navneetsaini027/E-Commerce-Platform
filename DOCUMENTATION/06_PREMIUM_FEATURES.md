## 🎯 How to Use New Features

### Spin the Wheel:
1. Look for the floating gift icon (bottom-right)
2. Click to open the wheel
3. Click "SPIN NOW" button
4. Wait for the wheel to stop
5. Get your discount code!
6. Come back tomorrow for another spin

### Quick View:
1. Hover over any product card
2. Click "Quick View" button (appears on hover)
3. View details in modal
4. Add to cart or wishlist
5. Close modal to continue browsing

### Recently Viewed:
1. Click on any product to view details
2. Scroll down to "Recently Viewed" section
3. See your browsing history
4. Click to revisit products

### Dark Mode:
1. Click Sun/Moon icon in navbar
2. Enjoy the starry night theme
3. See animated moon and stars
4. Toggle back anytime
